### What Is an MCP Server? (Simple Explanation)

MCP stands for **Model Context Protocol.**

Think of it as a USB-C port for your AI. Before MCP, every AI tool had its own plugin system. ChatGPT had plugins. Claude had tool use. Cursor had its own SDK. If you wanted your AI to connect to multiple apps, you needed to build four different integrations.

MCP fixes that. Write one server, and every AI client can use it.

Right now, MCP is supported by Claude Desktop, Claude Code, Cursor, Windsurf, VS Code, and more. The ecosystem crossed **97 million monthly SDK downloads** in early 2026.

That’s not a trend. That’s infrastructure.
